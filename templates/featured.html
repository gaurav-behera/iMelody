OneRepublic

Followers: 2.5M

        OneRepublic is an American pop rock band formed in Colorado Springs, Colorado in 2002. The band consists of lead vocalist and multi-instrumentalist Ryan Tedder, guitarist Zach Filkins, guitarist Drew Brown, bassist and cellist Brent Kutzle, and drummer Eddie Fisher. OneRepublic gained popularity with their debut single "Apologize" in 2007, which was a massive hit worldwide. Since then, they have released several successful albums and singles, including "Stop and Stare", "Good Life", "Counting Stars", and "Connection". OneRepublic is known for their catchy melodies, emotive lyrics, and Tedder's powerful vocals.
